Warranty Claim Process
Step 1: Issue Identification
Contact service center when issue arises
Step 2: Inspection
Authorized technician inspects the vehicle
Step 3: Claim Approval
Warranty coverage is verified and approved
Step 4: Repair/Replacement
Free repair or replacement under warranty

Vehicle returned with warranty updated
Important Information
What's Covered:
  • Manufacturing defects
  • Mechanical failures
  • Electrical malfunctions
  • Corrosion perforation
What's Not Covered:
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage from accidents
  • Lack of maintenance
  • Unauthorized modifications
Requirements:
  • Regular maintenance at authorized centers
  • Original parts only
  • Valid warranty card
  • Service history records
