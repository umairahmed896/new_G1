Frequently Asked Questions

Booking a test drive is easy:

  1. Browse our car catalogue and select your preferred vehicle
  2. Click on the "Book Test Drive" button
  3. Fill in your contact information and preferred date/time
  4. Submit the form and our team will contact you to confirm

You can also call our customer service at +92-300-1234567 to book a test drive.

We offer multiple financing options through our partner banks:

  • Up to 60% financing on new cars
  • Flexible tenure from 1 to 7 years
  • Competitive interest rates starting from 12%
  • Quick approval process
  • Minimal documentation required

Use our EMI calculator to estimate your monthly payments.

Tracking your order is simple:

  1. Visit our tracking page
  2. Enter your booking ID or order number
  3. View real-time status updates
  4. Get notifications on your phone and email

You can also track orders through our mobile app or by contacting customer service.

Our warranty coverage includes:

  • Engine and transmission components
  • Electrical and electronic systems
  • Manufacturing defects
  • Free service during warranty period

Warranty period varies by manufacturer - typically 2-3 years or 40,000-60,000 km, whichever comes first.

To cancel your booking:

  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to "My Bookings" section
  3. Select the booking you want to cancel
  4. Click on "Cancel Booking" and follow the instructions

Cancellation charges may apply. Please review our cancellation policy or contact customer service for assistance.
